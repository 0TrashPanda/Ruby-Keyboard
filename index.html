We wanted to see change in the keyboard space and make them more efficient.

Keyboards are the way they are because of the outdated limitations of typewriters, which no longer affect our technology. We want to change the primary way of interacting with your computer for the better!

Split keyboard

Compared to standard keyboards designs, split keyboards are more flexible and ergonomic.

Macro keys

Macros are commands or command chains that are called up by a key on the keyboard, they are fully customizable just like all other keys.

Thumbcluster

What finger has the most potential? - Right your thumb! Then why do we use it for only one key? That's just dumb.. That's why we have a thumb cluster.

Staggered column

Compared to the usual staggered row keyboards, ours is column staggered witch is more inline with the human hand.
